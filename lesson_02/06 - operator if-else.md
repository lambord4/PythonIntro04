# IF - ELSE

## Синтаксис
- block `IF` only
```
. . .
 
if <condition>:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N

. . .
```

- block `IF - ELSE`
```
. . .
 
if <condition>:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N
else:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N

. . .
```

- block `IF - ELIF - ELSE`
```
. . .
 
if <condition_1>:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N
elif <condition_2>:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N
elif <condition_3>:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N
else:
    operator_1
    operator_2
    .
    .
    .
    operator_N

. . .
```

- тернарный оператор
```
x = 5
a = 4 if x != 5 else x

'a' set 4 if 'x' not equal 5 else 'a' set 'x'
```
